Introducing the Simon Game

Here are the rules for the Simon game in a concise bullet-point format:

  • Watch and Remember: The game starts by lighting up a random quadrant with a sound.
  • Repeat the Sequence: Click the quadrants in the exact order they light up.
  • Progressive Difficulty: Each round adds a new step to the sequence.
  • Mistakes End the Game: If you click the wrong quadrant, the game resets.
  • Test Your Memory: Keep going as long as you can remember the sequence correctly!
